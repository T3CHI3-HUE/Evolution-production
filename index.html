ABOUT US

STEVE GERARD

Creative Director

Evolution Studios is an independent production company that covers all kinds of events, video and photography wise. We strive to help you relive exhilarating moments and give a detailed story to the world. We cover from Portrait photography to Street to Fashion to wedding photographs and food photography.
We also do video coverages of weddings, conferences, birthdays, product launches, anniversaries, burials, corporate events and many more. Furthermore, we offer both national and international services.
At Evolution Studios, our goal is to deliver exceptional service, thereby exceeding our client's expectations, while maintaining the highest standards of artistic integrity and professionalism. We value creativity, innovation, and collaboration.
We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, founded on trust, respect, and deep understanding of their needs.
